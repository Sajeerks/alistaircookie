Oatmeal Raisin Cookie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 3 cups of old-fashioned oats
  • 1 1/2 cups of raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla and honey.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into the sugar mixture until just incorporated.
  5. Mix in the oats and raisins until evenly distributed.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
  8. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
